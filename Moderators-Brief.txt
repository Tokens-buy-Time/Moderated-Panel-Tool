Briefing for discussion topic :
The Eastern Caribbean is perceived as being too small of a market in which to develop a General Aviation Industry that is sustainable.
Can Private Equity and adoption of new technologies, facilitate the development of business models that leverage Light Sport Aviation, to deliver successful commercial outcomes.
Such outcomes might involve, the establishment of FBO, MRO, Dealership facilities, on each Eastern and Southern Caribbean island, as well as in the South American nation of Guyana.
One might seek to discover, all of the assumptions and necessary input data that will allow one to evaluate the impact - upon IRR and ROI - for any likely franchise based business venture designed, developed and executed to operate within the region.
